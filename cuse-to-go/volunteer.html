Alpha Phi Omega

Alpha Phi Omega is a co-educational national service fraternity. Phi Chapter is located in Syracuse, NY and is open to Syracuse University (SU) and SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry (SUNY-ESF) students.


Adopt-A-Street

This is to provide Syracuse University student organizations with a long-term service project that will enhance the quality of life for students, facult and staff living off-campus and aid in the beautification of the off-campuse community.

We Rise Above the Streets

We Rise Above the Streets delivers high energy messages that encourage people to live up to their full potential by breaking the cycles of homelessness and crime many face daily.
